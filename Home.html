What make's SHA-CONVEYANCE special?

SHA-CONVEYANCE is a platform that connects travelers and locals farmers and street vendors selling seasonal fruits and fresh produce vegetables. It features a directory of street stalls selling fruits and vegetables, and a grocery shop where local farmers can sell their freshly picked goods. This makes it a convenient way to find delicious, healthy food while supporting local farmers.

Are you also craving a delicious snack or fresh produce? SHA-CONVEYANCE is your one-stop shop. Our platform lets you easily locate nearby street food stalls and order fresh farm goods for delivery.
